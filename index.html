Privacy Policy — Ascend

Last updated: 2026

Ascend is a personal discipline and habit-tracking app. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data we collect

None. Ascend does not have a server and does not send your data anywhere. Everything you enter — habits, streaks, challenges, photos, notes, and settings — is stored only on your own device, inside your browser/app storage.

Where your data lives

All data is saved locally on your device using on-device storage (IndexedDB). We cannot see it, access it, or recover it. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, your information is permanently deleted.

AI features

If you choose to use the optional AI planning feature and provide your own API key from a provider (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google), your requests go directly from your device to that provider under their own privacy policy. Ascend does not store, transmit, or see your API key beyond keeping it on your device so the feature can work.

Permissions

Ascend may ask permission to show notifications (for habit and event reminders). You can decline or revoke this at any time in your device settings. The app does not access your contacts, location, or microphone.

Children

Ascend is a general wellness and productivity tool. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact the developer through the app's Play Store listing.
